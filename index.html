What is TULI7?

TULI7 is a lightweight training tool for simulating battlefield effects.

Trainer can create ARTY, MINES and CBRN effects on a map. Players receive vibration and sound cues and learn to recognize effects without relying on the screen.

Purpose: improve understanding of modern battlefield effects in a simple, mobile-first way. Critical alerts can also appear as lock screen notifications.

Safety and privacy

TULI7 is a training and simulation tool. It does not model real battlefield effects exactly and does not replace official safety procedures or command.

Use only exercise identifiers such as BLUE 1 or RED 2. Do not use real names.

Session data, player state and exercise location may be processed during the exercise. Sessions are intended to expire automatically and are not meant for long-term storage.
